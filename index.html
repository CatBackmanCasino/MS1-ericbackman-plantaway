PRODUCTS

pSPHEAR

Our most simple yet sophisticated system
Perfect for shorter trips
Fill the sphear with water and push the sharp edge 2-3" into the soil.
Comes in pack of 4 pSphears


Capacity:
Up to 7 days

pDROP

System suitable for up to TEN plants.
Simply fill the 10 liter bag with water
Hang it slightly higher than the pots and put one of the tubes just a tad down into the soil. 10 tubes - 10 plants

Capacity:
Up to 20 days

pSTRING

Have many small plants?
This easy-to-use system is so easy it will blow your mind.
Place the water container on the same level as the pots.
Put one end of the specially designed strings in the container and the other 2-3" into the soil using the digger (included)
The string will lead the water to the plant as the soil dries.

Capacity:
Up to 14 days
30 days with a larger container

About

Going away on vacation is a wonderful time. For many of us that is when we create our best memories of our lives. Coming home to a sea of dead plants is one of the worst.

Plants are awesome. Even the most urban city slickers can appreciate the shades of green, the sense of life and the clean air they bring. They give us a lot and the only thing they want in return is a little water and sunlight. Well, a little more at times but that’s basically it.

But if you’re not there, how will they survive?

options?

  • You could ask someone to water them for you
    • They might not show up?
    • Can you trust them with a key?
    • They might water too much and kill your plants
  • OR you could find the RIGHT watering system for your needs and be sure your plants get what they need while you don’t have to worry about a thing

    • Sounds complicated?


    It’s not.
