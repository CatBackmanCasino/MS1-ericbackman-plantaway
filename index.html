About us:

We think going away on vacation is a wonderful time. For many of us that is when we create our best memories of our lives. Coming home to a sea of dead plants is one of the worst.
Plants are awesome. Even the most urban city slickers can appreciate the shades of green, the sense of life and the clean air they bring. They give us a lot and the only thing they want in return is a little water and sunlight. Well, a little more at times but that's basically it.

But if you're not there, how will they survive?

  • You could ask someone to water them for you
    • They might not show up?
    • Can you trust them with a key?
    • They might water too much and kill your plants
  • OR you could find the RIGHT watering system for your needs and be sure your plants get what they need while you don't have to worry about a thing

Our patented products each have different advantages. Take a quick look and you will know. If it takes you more than three minutes, give us a call or send us an e-mail and we will help you with the choice free of charge.

Good luck! /Plantcation Crew
